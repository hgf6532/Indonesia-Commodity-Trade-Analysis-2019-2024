# Indonesia-Commodity-Trade-Analysis-2019-2024
An Exploratory Data Analysis of Indonesian international trade flows spanning 2019-2024.
The analysis identifies structural patterns with material implications for regional and global supply chains. 
With a dive into how Indonesia occupies a critical upstream position in several of the world's most strategically sensitive supply chains — ranging from energy (coal) and agricultural commodities (palm oil) to the emerging clean-energy supply chain (nickel for EV batteries).
